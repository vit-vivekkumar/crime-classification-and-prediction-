About Us

To be better prepared to respond to criminal activity, it is important to understand patterns in crime. In our project, we analyze crime data from the city of Indore, scraped from publicly available website of Indore Police. At the outset, the task is to predict which category of crime is most likely to occur given a time and place in Indore.
